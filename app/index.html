About

VMaps Company, is a rapidly developing prospective GIS organization with extensive expertise that includes the following:

  • Vectorising raster maps with various degrees of detail and accuracy
  • Converting print and digital maps to a vector format
  • Managing field teams whom obtain the necessary data for creating vector maps
  • Designing and Maintaining Infographics
  • Designing Thematic maps
  • GIS Data Analysis

In addition, we are also looking forward to various other projects employing GIS Technology. We are open to other projects.
